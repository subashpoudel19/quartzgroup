Seminar on Energy Measurement for SPT at Pulchowk Campus

Mr. Subash Poudel delivered a lecture titled "SPT Measurement for SPT" at Department of Civil Engineering(Pulchowk Campus). The event organized by Nepal Geotechnical Society in association with the students' Civil Engineering Club. In his lecture, Mr. Subash Poudel emphasized on the need to standardize the Standard Penetration Test (SPT), measure the energy transferred using modern instrumentation techniques. He presented case studies highlighting the benefits.
