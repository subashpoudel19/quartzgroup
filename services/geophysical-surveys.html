Geophysical Surveys

Geophysical surveys are archaeological methods that use ground-based physical sensing techniques to produce a detailed image or map of an area. These methods are neither invasive nor destructive, an important goal when surveying culturally-sensitive sites such as cemeteries.

Our in-house Geophysical Surveying capabilities include:

  • Electrical resistivity imaging (ERI) and tomography
  • Ground-penetrating radar (GPR)
  • Spectral Analysis of Surface Waves (SASW)
  • Multi-Channel Analysis of Surface Waves (MASW)
