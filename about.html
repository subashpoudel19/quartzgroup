ABOUT US

Quartz is one of the leading Geotechnical Engineering Consulting firms in Nepal. Established in 2015, and led by a team of Tribhuvan University graduates, we have successfully completed over 200 projects across Nepal.

Powered by over half a decades of technical expertise and experience, we, at Quartz, are fully adept at conducting the full range of ground investigations, data review & interpretation, and engineering analyses pertaining to the Geo-technical domain. 

With a line-up of state-of-the-art equipment and other allied resources, we provide a comprehensive suite of services, which include Geotechnical Investigations, Topographical and Hydrographical Survey, Laboratory Testing, Foundation Testing, Foundation Construction, and Geological services.

Our organization has experienced significant growth, transitioning from a small startup to a reputable professional establishment. With a dedicated team of over 10 highly motivated individuals, we have become known for our commitment to offering engineering solutions to intricate geotechnical and geological challenges. Our focus extends to developing economically viable and secure designs for foundations and soil-related issues. Additionally, we possess expertise in the field of geological works, including Electric Resistivity Tomography (ERT) and data interpretation. Our project portfolio encompasses a range of notable ventures such as National Pride Hydropower projects, transmission lines, residential and commercial buildings, and more.


At Quartz, we consider "QUALITY" as more than just a requirement imposed on our staff by the company management; it is a habitual pursuit that we actively engage in on a daily basis. We understand the significance of our work in nation-building and our responsibility towards society. With the motto "We test today for tomorrow's progress," we are committed to making a positive impact and contribute to the advancement of society.
