ABOUT US

Cengrs is one of the leading Geotechnical Engineering Consulting firms in India. Established in 1990, and led by a team of IIT Delhi graduates, we have successfully completed over 6500 projects across India and abroad.

Powered by over three decades of technical expertise and experience, we, at Cengrs, are fully adept at conducting the full range of ground investigations, data review & interpretation, and engineering analyses pertaining to the Geo-technical domain. 

With a line-up of state-of-the-art equipment and other allied resources, we provide a comprehensive suite of services, which include Geotechnical Investigations, Topographical and Hydrographical Survey, Laboratory Testing, Foundation Testing, Foundation Construction, and Ground Improvement.

We have grown from a small startup to a professional organization with more than 150 highly motivated employees. We have established our reputation as a company committed to providing engineering solutions to complex geotechnical problems as well as developing economic and safe designs for foundations and soil-related problems. Our project line-up includes major thermal power plants, refineries, bridges & highways, commercial & residential infrastructure, metro & railways, etc.
We are represented in several national forums such as Indian Geotechnical Society, Deep Foundation Institute, Association of Consulting Civil Engineers, Indian Roads Congress and Indian Society of Rock Mechanics & Tunneling Technology. Our directors have served these forums in various capacities. Our directors are also members of the technical committees of the International Society for Soil Mechanics and Geotechnical Engineering, Bureau of Indian Standards and Indian Roads Congress and have been instrumented in drafting various codes and policies related to geotechnical engineering, piling, fly ash utilization, etc.

For us at Cengrs, “QUALITY” is a habit, to actively pursue on a routine basis, not something that is unwillingly imposed upon its staff by the company management. We recognize our commitment to the society at large and are aware of the implications of our work in nation building. We work with the motto “We test today for tomorrow’s progress”.

Mr. Sanjay Gupta

Managing Director

Started the ‘Fengrs’ Group in 1983 and “Cengrs Geotechnica Pvt. Ltd.” in 1990. A post graduate in Geotechnical Engineering with 47 years of experience, he is widely respected as one of the top Geotechnical Engineers in the country. Mr. Gupta has executed major projects of soil investigation, pile foundations and ground improvement across the country.

He is the key person in the company and controls all technical, contractual, financial and commercial matters. His expertise includes large diameter bored piles, high capacity load testing of piles, ground improvement using stone columns, foundation design under difficult soil conditions including soft ground/ weak soils and under artesian conditions. Professionally, he is committed to bridge the gap between academic research and field practice annd strives to infuse and implement modern practices in the Indian industry. He is recognized as a leader in the geotechnical investigation field in the country.
