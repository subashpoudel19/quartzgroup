ABOUT US

Quartz is one of the leading Geotechnical Engineering Consulting firms in Nepal. Established in 2015, and led by a team of Tribhuvan University graduates, we have successfully completed over 200 projects across Nepal.

Powered by over half a decades of technical expertise and experience, we, at Quartz, are fully adept at conducting the full range of ground investigations, data review & interpretation, and engineering analyses pertaining to the Geo-technical domain. 

With a line-up of state-of-the-art equipment and other allied resources, we provide a comprehensive suite of services, which include Geotechnical Investigations, Topographical and Hydrographical Survey, Laboratory Testing, Foundation Testing, Foundation Construction, and Geological services.

We have grown from a small startup to a professional organization with more than 10 highly motivated employees. We have established our reputation as a company committed to providing engineering solutions to complex geotechnical and geological problems as well as developing economic and safe designs for foundations and soil-related problems. In addition, our expertise lies in the area of geological works like Electric Resistivity Tomograph (ERT) and interpreting the data. Our project line-up includes National Pride Hydropower projects, transmission lines, residential and commercial buildings, etc.

For us at Quartz, “QUALITY” is a habit, to actively pursue on a routine basis, not something that is unwillingly imposed upon its staff by the company management. We recognize our commitment to the society at large and are aware of the implications of our work in nation building. We work with the motto “We test today for tomorrow’s progress”.
