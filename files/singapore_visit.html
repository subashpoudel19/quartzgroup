Workshop on Surface exploration by Seismic methods, Singapore, 2023

Publish : Sept 2022, 08
Previous Next
Mr. Kapil Karki visited singapore to attend a workshop

The workshop was titled Latest Geophysical Techniques for Infrastructure Projects organized by AF Academy (in association with American Concrete Institute, Singapore Chapter and technology partners M/s guideline Geo and M/s screening Eagle) on April 13-14, 2023 in Singapore.

The various technical trainings provided in the workshop are:

  • Brief History, Present & Future Trends in Geophysics by Mr. Jimmy Adcock, ABEM Product Manager, Guideline Geo
  • Latest Trends in 3D Processing of GPR by Mr Augustine Jiang, Application Manager, Screening Eagle
  • Seismic Methods-I (Seismic Refraction, MASW/ ReMi): Dr. Sanjay Rana
  • Seismic Methods-II (Crosshole/ Crossface Seismic Tomography, Crosshole/ downhole/ Uphole Seismic): Dr. Sanjay Rana
  • Electrical Resistivity Imaging/ Tomography: Dr. Sanjay Rana
  • Time Domain Electromagnetic: Mr. Jimmy Adcock
  • Processing & Interpretation of Geophysical Data (Electrical Resistivity, Imaging Ground Penetrating Radar)
  • Processing & Interpretation of Geophysical Data (Seismic Refraction & Seismic Refraction Tomography, MASW, Seismic Tomography, Crosshole Seismic
